Steven Paul (Steve) Jobs (San Francisco, 24 februari 1955 – Palo Alto, Californië, 5 oktober 2011) was the co-founder and CEO of Apple and a member of the board of directors of Pixar Animation Studios.

The career of Steve Jobs

  • 1976 - 1985 Apple Inc. - Co-Founder
  • 1997 - 2011 Apple Inc. - Chief Executive Officer
  • 1986 - 2006 Pixar - Co-Founder and Chief Executive Officer
  • 1985 - 1996 NeXT - Founder and Chief Executive Officer
